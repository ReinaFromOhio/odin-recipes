White Chicken Chili

A delectable and easy to make slow-cooked white chicken chili combining all the flavors of the southwest with all the comforts of home.


Ingredients

  • 4 cans of white beans (a combination of cannellini and great northern make a good pairing)
  • 2 cans southwest-style corn
  • 2 packets white chicken chili mix
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1.5lbs chicken thighs
  • 1 large onion, chopped and sauted
  • 2 blocks cream cheese

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients but cream cheese in slow cooker and cook on low for 5-6 hours (or until chicken is fully cooked and tender)
  2. Remove chicken and shread
  3. Replace chicken and add two blocks cream cheese
  4. Resume cooking on low until cream cheese is fully melted
  5. Serve with garnish of fresh cilantro and lime wedge
